GrabCoinTap guide

GrabCoin Tap is an exciting play-to-earn game where every tap brings coins and unlocks new levels. Enjoy thrilling gameplay, accumulate in-game coins, and exchange them for MetaMind Metaverse tokens or use them to craft powerful items and NFTs.

How to Play?

Tap and collect coins

Touch the screen to gather coins
The more taps, the more coins you accumulate

Advance through levels that become increasingly challenging and engaging
With each level, the number of coins you can collect increases

HOW TO DO MORE?

HOW TO DO MORE?

invite friends

For each friend who joins, you'll receive 5000 coins. Just share the link, and when your friend joins, the bonus is yours!

how it works
  1. Copy the link and send it to your friend
  2. For each friend who joins using your link, you'll receive 5000 coins, and your friend will get a friendly bonus of 2500 coins
  3. Track your friends' progress in the game and earn bonuses for their achievements
Bonus for levels

Earn extra coins when your friends progress through new levels. The more they play, the more coins you accumulate! Click on a friend to see all of their friends up to 5 levels deep. Each level is highlighted in a different color. This way, you can track your friends' progress in the game, see how many coins they've collected, and which level they're currently on

Tasks

Want to earn more coins? Check out the "Tasks" section! There will be exciting challenges, each offering generous rewards Each task is a new challenge and opportunity! Simply click on a task to find out what needs to be done, complete it, and earn coins Remember: you can never have too many coins!

Farms

Owning any farm accelerates crystal harvesting. There are six farms in total, each with its own cost and each mining different types of crystals, varying in value. Crystals can be exchanged for coins or kept for use in MetaMind. They are useful for crafting powerful items, protective fields, and other upgrades.

Tell your friends about the value of farms, and for each farm purchase made by your friend, you'll receive a bonus in the currency they used for the purchase (except for the StoneBounty farm, which does not provide a bonus for referring friends).
